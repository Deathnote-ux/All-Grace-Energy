Professor Adegbulugbe

CEO

He is a senior professor of Economics at the University of Ibadan and the director for Centre of Petroleum, Energy Economics and Law (CPEEL), a John D. Catherine T. MacArthur Foundation Regional Centre for Excellence in the teaching and research on energy studies and law university of Ibadan. Professor Adeola Adenikinju attended the University of Ibadan for his bachelor’s degree in 1983-1987. He went further to study for his masters and doctorate degree in economic also at the prestigious University of Ibadan from 1988-1994. Professor Adeola Adenikinju has received several distinctions and awards. He was part of the technical advisers to the presidential committee on the deregulation of the downstream petroleum sector 2009. He was the chairman for the finance working group on Nigeria Renewable Energy masterplan in 2015. Professor Adeola Adenikinju was nominated by the Guardian as “Distinguished Professionals 2015”. He was the coordinator of the first operational plan; the Nigeria Integrated Infrastructure Master Plan in the energy sector in 2017. He has fellowship awards from the Nigerian Association for Energy Economics (fnaee), energy institute, London and so on. Professor Adeola Adenikinju is a member of the Central Bank of Nigeria Monetary Policy Committee. He became the chairman of energy policy committee in 2015 till date. He is a member of the International Association for Energy Economics, USA. Professor Adeola Adenikinju has been consulted widely for several national and international bodies on renewable energy and Oil & Gas related issues. He was a consultant to UNECA- south regional on renewable energy. He has published and presented several books and papers to international journals.
